Refreshing Summer Berry Pop

Healthy, sweet and super refreshing on a hot summer's day. Enjoy an ice cold popsicle without the guilt!

Makes 6 Popsicles

INGREDIENTS

- 7 to 8 Large Strawberries

- 1/4 cup blueberries

- 1/4 cup orange juice

- 1 tsp maple syrup

PREPARATION

Wash all produce. Begin by cutting the strawberries into halves, removing their green tops.

Into a highspeed blender, add strawberries, blueberries, orange juice and maple syrup. Blend until smooth.

Pour into popsicle molds or into an ice cube tray (these work great as ice cubes in a fruity punch!). Allow to freeze overnight before enjoying!
