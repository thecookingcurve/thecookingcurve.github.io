Tropical Nice Cream

Made creamy with the use of frozen bananas, this tropical "nice" cream is so deliciously creamy without any cream!

Makes four small portions

INGREDIENTS

- 1 frozen banana (freeze in small chunks overnight or longer)

- 1 cup frozen pineapple (freeze in small chunks overnight or longer)

- 1 mango

- 1 tsp maple syrup

- 1/4 cup milk

PREPARATION

Add all ingredients into a highspeed blender. Blend until smooth.

Serve immediately for soft serve ice cream. Or freeze for 1 to 2 hours for a firmer ice cream.
