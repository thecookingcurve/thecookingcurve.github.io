WELCOME!

Hello there! Welcome to The Cooking Curve! My name is Yashvi and I started this blog to document some of the recipes I have curated over the years. I wanted to combine my passion for cooking and programming by creating this site. Throughout the process I developed skills in both culinary and web development fields.

Inspired by celebrity chefs on Food Network, I took to the kitchen to explore and experiment with recipes to create my favourite recipes. Recently, I've also developed a new passion, web development. Through online courseware I was able to self-teach myself the basics in HTML, CSS and Javascript. In many ways, cooking and programming are similar. There are tons of frustrating moments, lots of failures and times when you want to give up. But at the end of the day, when everything is finally working you are left with a pretty incredible (or delicious!) end result.
