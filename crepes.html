Easy Vegan Whole Wheat Crepes

Only four ingredients! Super easy vegan whole wheat crepes, that only take a few minutes to prep. Fill these crepes with sweet or savoury contents!

Makes 3 to 4 crepres

INGREDIENTS

- 1/2 cup whole wheat flour

- 3/4 cup almond milk

- 1/4 tsp baking powder

- 1/4 tsp salt

PREPARATION

Add the flour, baking powder and salt to a large deep bowl. Stir to combine.

Add half of the milk into the dry ingredients. Whisk, and then slowly add the remaining milk into the batter. Whisk till smooth. The batter should be thinner than pancake batter but thicker than regular crepe batter. The batter should run off a spoon quite easily and hold a 'S' shape in the batter for a few seconds.

On medium heat, warm up a small non-stick pan with a fat of your choice. Butter or a non-flavoured oil will work, use just enough to coat the bottom of the pan. Allow the pan to be coated in the fat, evenly. Wait around 3 minutes for the pan to heat up.

Add 1/4 cup of the batter at a time and swirl the pan to coat the entire surface. Cook until the top is set, and starts to pull away from the sides. Flip the crepe with the use of a spatula. Lower heat to low. Cook for another 2 to 3 minutes. Repeat steps 3 to 6 until you have used up all your batter.

Serve warm, and enjoy!
