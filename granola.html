Easy Healthy Vegan Granola

A super easy way to make your breakfasts a little more healthy. Ditch the sugary cereals and try making homemade granola instead.

Makes around 3 cups of granola.

INGREDIENTS

- 2 cups rolled oats

- 1/2 cup walnuts (chopped)

- 3 tbsp maple syrup

- 3 tbsp coconut oil

- 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds

- 2 tsp cinnamon

- 1 tsp salt

PREPARATION

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.

Melt coconut oil in a small bowl until it has melted completely. Add maple syrup and stir.

To a large bowl, add oats, walnuts, pumpkin seeds, cinnamon and salt and combine.

Add the oil and syrup mixture into the dry ingredients. Mix to combine.

Spread the oats on to a large sheet pan lined with parchment paper. Spread into an even layer

Bake for around 15 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
