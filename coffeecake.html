Italian Sponge Cake with Coffee Buttercream

Light, airy and full of great coffee flavour! This italian sponge cake with coffee buttercream is a great elegant dessert.

Serves Four

INGREDIENTS

CAKE

- 3 eggs

- 1/2 cup white sugar

- 1/2 cup AP flour

- 1/4 tsp baking soda

- 1/4 cup strong coffee

- 1/3 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

- 2 tsp coconut oil

COFFEE BUTTERCREAM

- 1/4 cup room temp unsalted butter

- 1/4 cup powdered sugar

- 2 tsp coffee powder, hot water and white sugar

PREPARATION

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.

In to a large mixing bowl, add three eggs. Mix on highspeed for 1 minute. Slowly begin to add the white sugar into the eggs. Beat on highspeed for 6 to 8 minutes. The mixture should turn a very pale yellow, and become very airy.

In a seperate bowl, add the flour and baking soda. Mix to combine. Using a spatula gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet. Only mix until the flour has combined.

Pour the batter into a 13 x 9 inch pan lined with parchment.

Bake for 15 to 20 minutes (until golden brown).

While the cake bakes, get started on the buttercream.

First add 2 tsp instant coffee, 2 tsp hot water and 2 tsp white sugar into a bowl. Use a whisk to beat the mixture until it has turned a lighter brown colour. This also known as Dalgona Coffee.

To a seperate bowl, beat 1/4 cup of butter on medium speed. Slowly add the powdered sugar and beat on medium speed for 5 to 6 minutes. Continue to mix on low speed for 1 to 2 minutes to incorporate the coffee mixture.

Also, melt the chocolate and coconut oil together in a small bowl.

When the cake comes out of the oven, allow it cool for 15 minutes.

Then, cut the cake into three even layers. Soak each layer with a generous layer of coffee, spooned on top (this keeps the cake moist).

Add a layer of buttercream then chocolate until you have reached the final layer. Cover the cake in the remaining chocolate and garish with slivered almonds. Enjoy!
