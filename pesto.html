Healthyish Pesto

Different than a classic pesto, this is a pesto that has great basil flavour yet is healthier version of the traditional.

INGREDIENTS

- 4 garlic cloves

- half one green chili

- 1/4 cup cashews

- 1 tsp lime zest

- 1 tbsp lime juice

- 2 tbsp olive oil

- 1/2 tsp baking soda

- 1/3 cup spinach (packed)

- 2/3 cup basil (packed)

- salt to taste

PREPARATION

In a food processor add all the ingredients, except the basil leaves. Pulse until almost smooth.

Add in basil, pulse until basil has combined. Salt to taste.

You can use this pesto on pasta, pizza, even on sandwiches! Enjoy!
