Summer Strawberry Crisp

Sweet, tart and warm. This summer strawberry crisp is a perfect summer treat. Paired with a basil-fused whipped cream, this dessert captializes on strawberry and basil working so well together!

Serves 4

INGREDIENTS

- 3 tbsp oats

- 1 tbsp AP flour

- 1 tbsp powdered sugar

- 1 tsp salt

- 1 tbsp olive oil + more to coat the pan

- 1 tsp cinnamon

- 5 large strawberries (cut into eighths)

- 1 tbsp orange juice

- 1 tsp cornstarch

PREPARATION

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.

Coat the bottom of a loaf pan with 1 tsp of olive oil.

To a small bowl, add strawberries, orange juice and cornstarch. Mix to combine.

Pour the strawberry mixture into the bottom of the pan.

Add the oats, flour, sugar, salt, cinnamon and olive oil to a separate bowl. Mix to combine. Evenly spread the crumble mixture on top of the strawberries.

Bake for 20 mins at 350 degrees. Allow to cool for 15 minutes.

Serve with ice cream or whipped cream.

*In the picture above, the strawberry crumble was served with basil infused whipped cream.
