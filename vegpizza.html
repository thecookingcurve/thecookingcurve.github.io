The Ultimate Veggie Pizza

This pizza calls out all of my vegetable lovers! LOL. But seriously, want a super delicious way to sneak in some of your favourite veggies?

Serves 4 Large Portions

INGREDIENTS

PIZZA DOUGH

- 1 and 1/2 cup all purpose flour

- 1 cup multigrain flour

- 1 cup warm water

- 2 tsp dry active yeast

- 2 tsp salt

- 1 tbsp sugar

- 1 tbsp olive oil

TOPPINGS

- Tomato Basil Pizza Sauce (or any pizza sauce will work!)

- 1 cup of shredded mozzarella

- 1/4 cup Parmesan

- Olive oil

- 5 cloves garlic

- 1 cup spinach (saut√©ed with garlic)

- 1/4 cup mushrooms (cooked)

- 1/4 cherry tomatoes

- 1 small onion

- 1/4 of a zucchini

- Handful of olives

- Red chili flakes

- Basil

- Salt

- Pepper

PREPARATION

MAKING THE DOUGH

Warm up the water in a tall glass until it is slightly warm to the touch. Add yeast and sugar then slightly stir. Let the yeast bloom for five minutes.

Add both flours and salt into a large bowl. Mix to combine. Slowly start adding the water into the flour, while constantly mixing. Once a dough is formed, use your hands to knead the dough for five minutes. Cover the dough in olive oil and rest for five hours at room temperature.

MAKING THE PIZZA

Preheat the oven to 415 degrees Fahrenheit.

Oil the bottom of a sheet pan with 2 tbsp of olive oil. Cover evenly. Dump the rested dough into the pan and start slowly pushing the dough to the edges of the pan. Do not try to force the dough to move.

Once the dough is too elastic and starts pushing back, rest for five minutes and try again. You want the dough to cover the length of the entire pan.

Add the mozzarella on top of the pizza (this is similar to a grandma pie).

Spoon the tomato sauce evenly over top of the cheese. Evenly distribute the rest of the toppings over the pizza.

Bake for 20 minutes or until golden brown. Serve immediately.
